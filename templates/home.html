Welcome to The Eye

The Cybersecurity Society of PSG Tech

The Eye is a cybersecurity-focused college society under the Computer Science and Engineering Association at PSG College of Technology. We have multiple verticals to help our members learn and practice cybersecurity in their everyday life. We organize events to increase awareness of Cybersecurity within the college, and also conduct activities like research, secure coding, and mock box attacks.

Join us for weekend events every Saturday, workshops, hackathons, and mega events at college.
